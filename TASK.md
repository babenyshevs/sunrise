* The Chief Consumer Officer is looking to reduce cancellation rates among fixed internet customers.
* The plan is to give a free Wi-Fi booster to every internet client, leading to better in-home connectivity and, hence, a more sticky product.

* To assess financials work with the following assumptions: 
	- The monetary value of a prevented cancellation is CHF 100 of monthly revenue for 18 months.
	- A booster's total unit cost (including shipping and installation) is CHF 35.
	- The total internet user base is 1.5 million customers.

* For the purpose of this exercise, you may ignore any timing, time-to-event and observation window related factors.
