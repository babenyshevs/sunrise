## Guardrails & risks
- **"How I used to run it" dynamic with Klaus** — he built this role and holds strong priors. Leverage him as the #1 context source; surface intended changes early and explicitly rather than diverging silently.
- **Don't over-fit the reactive→proactive thesis** — it's Klaus's framing and looks right, but validate with stakeholder evidence before reorganising work around it.
- **Matrix ambiguity** — reporting lines (esp. the Data CoE / Performance Analytics where [Vincie](../team/xi-vincie-wang.md) sits) must be clarified before assigning accountability.
- **Language** — English is the safe working language; several reports are German-native ([Nora](../team/nora-ballinari-bearth/nora-ballinari-bearth.md), [Christoph](../team/christoph-meier.md), Klaus). Keep improving German (A2→) as a rapport bridge, not a delivery dependency.
- **Credibility comes from listening first** — resist proving value through early technical heroics; the team is capable and the win is in framing, standards, and decisions.
